Quotes on [[silence]].

<blockquote class='font-serif text-justify leading-relaxed align-middle'>
  When I am liberated by silence, when I am no longer involved in the measurement of life, but in the living of it, I can discover a form of prayer in which there is effectively no distraction. My whole life becomes a prayer. My whole silence is full of prayer. The world of silence in which I am immersed contributes to my prayer.
</blockquote>
<p class='font-serif text-right pr-4 -mt-4'>
  — Thomas Merton, <a href="https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/45763868" target="_blank"><em>Thoughts in Solitude</em></a><br/>
</p>

<blockquote class='font-serif text-justify leading-relaxed align-middle'>
  Wovon man nicht sprechen kann, darüber muss man schweigen.<br/>
  (Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent.)
</blockquote>
<p class='font-serif text-right pr-4 -mt-4'>
  — Ludwig Wittgenstein, <a href="https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/1086999432" target="_blank"><em>Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus</em></a><br/>
  (Translated by <a href="https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/1120937278" target="_blank">C.K. Ogden</a>)
</p>
